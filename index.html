Product Focus and Latest News

New Mini-Scanner Systems:

Mini-scanners complement our existing multi-enclosure scanner technology but are designed for smaller monitoring areas, providing both corrosion and heat flux monitoring and mapping capabilities. All the mini-scanner electronics is housed in a single easy-install enclosure.

New Heat Flux and Corrosion (HFC) Monitors:

These low-cost, stand-alone units are designed for both heat flux and corrosion monitoring at point locations on boiler membrane walls and other surfaces. They use the principle of electrical resistance measurement to accurately determine the real heat flux and any loss-in-thickness due to corrosion or erosion.

Sensors are simply welded to external tube walls as opposed to intrusive heat flux systems that require tube removal and replacement.

Industrial Monitoring

We design and supply a range of systems for monitoring both the integrity (corrosion, erosion and cracking) and thermal behaviour of industrial plants. Our scanner technology, designed to monitor and map large areas of plant such as boiler walls, has been used in power generation plant since 1999. 'Mini-scanners' now complement these larger systems and are designed for smaller monitoring areas. New heat flux and corrosion monitors are designed for precision monitoring at point locations.

Protection & Conservation

We provide services and systems for corrosion risk assessment, prevention and control, and specialise in the design and supply of cathodic protection systems to inhibit the corrosion of embedded metalwork in masonry and concrete.
