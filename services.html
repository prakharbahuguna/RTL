Services

Based in Manchester, North West England, Rowan Technologies provides a diverse range of services related to corrosion engineering and materials performance to both UK and overseas clients.

Corrosion Consultancy, Materials Performance and Failure Analysis

Using our highly qualified staff, we provide specialist services in corrosion consultancy, prevention, monitoring and control. We undertake failure analysis and investigations caused by corrosion or mechanical means. The company can also carry out trials of metals and alloys under specialised service conditions and can advise on materials selection and performance.

Environmental Monitoring

We carry out environmental monitoring of buildings and structures to investigate general degradation and corrosion-related problems. This enables specific advice to be given as to how best to conserve the building fabric. We provide services for buildings and structures ranging from the historic to new-build.

Materials Testing, Analysis and Evaluation

For materials performance, testing and evaluation, Rowan Technologies has access to a wide range of test and measurement facilities from environmental test chambers to sophisticated analytical instruments.

Industrial Plant and Thermal Monitoring

We carry out corrosion/integrity and process monitoring in all types of industrial plant.
